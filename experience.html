Experience

One thing that differentiates us from most museums is that we not only want to educate you about golf history and technology, but also provide a full immersive experience. We have a large selection of limited edition drivers, woods, irons, wedges, and putters that you can try out. You can play on all 5 of the courses we have displays on with our clubs on our simulators. We also have an indoor putting and chipping area.
