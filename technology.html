Technology

California has also contributed a significant amount to the technology and development of golf. The majority of the top golf brands in the world are located in California, including Titleist, Callaway, Taylormade, Cobra, Odyssey, Scotty Cameron, Cleveland, and many more. We have displays for you to learn about the biggest technological advances in golf history. Titleist revolutionized golf balls in 2000 when it released the Pro V1. In the 1980s Scotty Cameron perfected the putter by refining popular designs. He has since won several awards and now works with Titleist. In 1988 Cleveland set the standard for all modern wedges with their 588. Callaway changed the game with the first oversized driver, the Big Bertha.
