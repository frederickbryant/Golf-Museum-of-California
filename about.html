Our Story

Golf has by far the largest amount of potential players compared to other sports. You can essentially play it for your entire life. Golf is a very exciting sport and one of the most fun to play once people give it a chance. Unfortunately many people including myself, saw it as dull until I started to play. Golf is now much more affordable and accessible to anyone, but unfortunately the demographics haven't changed much. The goal of this museum is to make more people appreciate golf and they would be more willing to engage in the sport. By coming to the museum people will have the opportunity to play golf if they didn't previously have the opportunity to head out to a course. Also, this museum provides history and the technological design of golf.
